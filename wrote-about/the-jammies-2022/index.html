The Jammies 2022.

To my surprise this website was recently nominated for the "Personal Website of the Year" award at Jamstack Conf 2022! To be nominated as a finalist in this category, both by a public vote and alongside two seriously creative websites from John Beresford and Lynn Fisher, is a real honour.

I wanted to write a quick post to say a huge thank you to anyone and everyone who nominated or voted for this website. This site isn't just a personal website, it's also the online face of my business, and the recognition that this kind of event brings is such a huge boost. Cheers, you're awesome! üçª

Most importantly of all though, I want to say a massive congratulations to Lynn Fisher for bringing home the win. Lynn's annual website update is always super creative, and I'm stoked to see that she picked up a very well-deserved golden jam jar. Lynn's work is a constant source of inspiration for me, and I can't wait to see what the next iteration of her site brings!
